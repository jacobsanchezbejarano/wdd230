Santa Cruz is the first department in recovering economic activity until levels it has before pandemic

President of the state said Santa Cruz economic activity grew more than 5.7% this year, “With this growth, Santa Cruz becomes the first department to recover and exceed the levels of economic activity it had before the pandemic. Congratulations, Santa Cruz!” the authority highlighted. He added that until July of this administration, the department's exports increased by 63%, driven mainly by the manufacturing industry and the extraction of hydrocarbons.
