Audience

Our audience are business owners that seek for interpersonal conections with people arround the city, in order to get better providers, clients and professional relationships. Also, they are interested in attend international speeches about industrial technology and international commerce.

Persona CIAGRO Sub-Manager
Photo
Fictional Name Juan Carlos Medrano
Job Title/Major Responsibilities Sub-Manager
Demographics
  • 35 years old
  • Married
  • Father of two children
  • Has a Master's degree in Agricultural Business
Goals and tasks He is focused, goals-oriented. His task is to maintain a good relationship with providers and achieve a fluid supply chain.
Environment He uses a computer every day, for job reports, news, email communication with providers. Also, he uses WhatsApp, Facebook and Instagram.
Quote "Can you send me the prices of the detailed products by this afternoon?"

Scenarios

The following scenarios, describe what the users would expect to see in the website.

  • I would like to know some people to work in the last project I developed.
  • I would like to know some providers of agricultural supplies.
  • I would like to register in the Chamber of Commerce of Santa Cruz.
  • Where could I find some speech about international supply chain? I feel outdated.
  • I would like to make more business conections, where could I find some social events for business men?
